**Quick pronunciation tips:**
- **ǐ/ǐ** = rising-falling tone (like saying "really?" skeptically)
- **è** = falling tone, like a grunt "eh"
- **ù** = falling tone
- **ā** = flat, high tone
- **X** in pinyin ≈ "sh" but softer, almost "sy"
- **Zh** ≈ like "j" in "jump"
- **Q** in pinyin ≈ "ch" but lighter
