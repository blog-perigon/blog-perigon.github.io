### 🔤 Quick pronunciation tips

* **ǐ / ǐ** = dipping tone (like saying *“really?”* skeptically)
* **è** = sharp falling tone, like a quick “eh!”
* **ù** = strong falling tone
* **ā** = flat, high tone (hold it steady)
* **X (xi)** ≈ soft “sh” (tongue forward, lighter)
* **Zh** ≈ like “j” in *jump*
* **Q** ≈ light “ch” (like *cheese* but softer)
* **C** ≈ “ts” (like *cats*)
* **Z** ≈ “dz”
