### 📱 **China Survival (Lock Screen)**

**💬 Basics**

* 你好 — *Ni hao* (Hello)
* 谢谢 — *Shieh-shieh* (Thanks)
* 不 — *Boo* (No)
* 对 — *Dway* (Yes)

**💰 Shopping**

* 这个多少钱？ — *Juh-guh dwo-shao?* (How much?)
* 太贵了！ — *Tai gway luh!* (Too expensive!)
* 我要买这个 — *Wo yow my juh-guh* (I want this)

**📍 Getting Around**

* …在哪里？ — *… dzai na-lee?* (Where is…?)
* 火车站 — *Hwo-che jan* (Train station)
* 酒店 — *Jyo-dyen* (Hotel)

**🌶️ Food**

* 不要辣！ — *Boo yow la!* (No spicy!)

**📶 Internet & Payment**

* WiFi密码？ — *WiFi mee-ma?* (WiFi password?)
* 我需要WiFi用支付宝 —
  *Wo shoo-yow WiFi, Alipay*
  (Need WiFi for Alipay)

**🆘 Backup**

* 我不明白 — *Wo boo ming-bye* (Don’t understand)
