After going to Iran from Germany in 2022, and Iraq from Norway in 2023, countless other trips to Africa and The Middle East - I needed a new challenge -> I wanted to do something difficult again.

So here is the premise: I am going to China on the surface of the earth - It will be about 12.000 km crossing vast parts of asia.

I am allowed to take any vehicle that is not particularly comissioned for me, any vehicle that would run the same route without me.

So, valid modes of transport include: hitch hiking an already running car, taking a train, a bus, marshrutka, microavtobus, dolmus, miànbāochē.
