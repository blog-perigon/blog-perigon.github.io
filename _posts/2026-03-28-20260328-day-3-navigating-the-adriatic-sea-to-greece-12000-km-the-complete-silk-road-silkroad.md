The Day started in Bari, with a regional train to Brindisi. Ever since my childhood I wanted to take a ferry from there (imagine a head tip to Jules Vernes' "In 80 Days around the World" :)

The weather was kind and the sunset on the Adriatic sea was beautiful. Found a small hotel after arrival in Igoumenitsa - Zigos Holiday, wonderful garden + nice people.

Tomorrow I will take the bus to Athens/Piraeus Port.
