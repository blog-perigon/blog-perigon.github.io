I am still stuck in Georgia, and have not got the permission to enter Azerbaijan on a land border, though I decided to go to Tbilisi in case I get it before the Orthodox Easter.

Due to the landborder being closed there is no public transport to the border, not even close to it, the closest I will get on a bus or marshrutka from Tbilisi is 30 km from the checkpoint.

Will have to walk and hitch hike.
