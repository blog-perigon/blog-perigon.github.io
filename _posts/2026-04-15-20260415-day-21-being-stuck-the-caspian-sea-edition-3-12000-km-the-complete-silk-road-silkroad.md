I spent my whole day within a circle of about 200m radius, the anchor chain is less then 50m, due to the shifting wind the ship almost completed a full circle.

We can see Baku, Azerbaijan at a distance of about 15km.

While there is a storm at our destination that topples over trucks we have wonderfully sunny and warm weather, some people improve their tan while others go fishing.

There are a lot of truck drivers aboard. I played Domino with them and used the time to learn backgammon and durak (a card game).

Together with 3 meals a day, what more can i ask for?
