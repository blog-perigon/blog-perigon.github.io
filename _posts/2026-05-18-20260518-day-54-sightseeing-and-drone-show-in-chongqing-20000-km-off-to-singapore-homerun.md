Still recovering from whatever I had in Taklamakan and Tibet I spent half of the day in bed and sight seeing in the afternoon and evening.

May 4th was the last day the drone show was on display, so went to a place where it could be seen well. On the opposite riverbank would have even been better, but that was too far away and not reachable by walking.
